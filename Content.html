Since the Philippines only has 2 weathers, which is hot and cold/cool, I have some
Recommendations!


1. Club de nuit (EDT, EDP, PARFUM)

Club de nuit is a dupe/clone of Creed Aventus that costs around (P15k - P20k). For a clone it smells amazing and smells extremely close to the original . It lasts longer and projects farther than Aventus which is a huge selling point for others.



×

2. Symmetry Labs Marksman (EDP)

Here we have an inspired frag made here in the Philippines. It's inspired from another niche fragrance namely Parfums De Marly herod. It has a clean tobacco and vanilla scent blended together evening out the tobacco scent. It smells really close to PDM's Herod, so if you're looking for a cheaper alternative and still get the same scent profile, go with this one.

×


3. Sauvage Elixir


Sauvage Elixir is a beast mode fragrance that has strong sillage and projection. If you want to be noticed and make your existence known, this frag makes it so. You can wear it during cold weathers as it's smell goes well under cold weater. 2 sprays MAX... Seriously..

×


4. Man in Black

Man in Black by Bvlgari as stated by the name, is best worn going out in black tie events, and cold weather. It has the same scent profile as Spicebomb and as it dries down it transitions to the scent of Spicebomb Extreme. The spices, the vanilla and rum compliments each other and has a great dry down, you should spray this one on your skin than clothes to really flush the scents out.

×


5. Terrae Essence

Terrae Essence is another perfume by Bvlgari, if you want to smell like the earth with a more citrusy touch, defintely get this.Do note It is not blind buy worthy as the smell may be too strong for you as this perfume is not for everyone. It has strong projection and sillage i recommend 2-3 sprays, definitely in the cold weather.


×


6. Hugo Boss Bottled Night

Bottled night is a great night time fragrance, it has a dark, woody notes making it ideal for date nights, going out at night. This is due to it not trying too hard to get attention, but it gets the job done.


×


7. Montale Arabians Tonka

Arabians Tonka by Montale doesn't beat around the bush just like sauvage elixir, it's really really strong, really sweet, and lasts really long. We're talking about 8+ hrs here. Tested it with only 1 spray as per the initial reviews I have seen, with just 1 spray everyone around the house can smell me even when I'm downstairs. 2 sprays MAX, perfect for cold weathers.






It's important to consider the event, and environment you go and choose the appropriate perfume, unless you're one of the people that has their own rules but hey more power to you my friend. Stay fresh!
